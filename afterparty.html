More Information Coming Soon!

If you have friends or family who want to meet you at the After Party, there will be free shuttles from Justin Herman Plaza leaving between 7:30am – 8:45am to take them to the After Party at Marina Green. The pre-race shuttles will be lined up on the west side of the Embarcadero, right in front of Justin Herman Plaza, across from the Ferry Building. Post-race shuttle buses will bring participants and their friends/families back from Marina Green to Justin Herman Plaza between 10:00am – 2:00pm.

Each registered race participant can show their bib at one of the After Party bars to pick up their free beer! (One per registered race participant, please). Additional beers can be purchased as well.

Food trucks will be parked in parking spots in the lot adjacent to Marina Green if you’re hungry. Check back for updates on what food trucks will be participating.

There will be a live band at the After Party! Check back soon for updates on what band will be playing.

This race and after party will happen rain or shine. Plan accordingly and pack layers.
