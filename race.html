Course Information

Course

Both courses start at 9am downtown at the Ferry Building in the shadow of the Bay Bridge. Run through Fisherman's Wharf, along the Marina Green where the 5K finishes. The 12K continues on out and back through Crissy Field with magnificent views of the Golden Gate Bridge finishing at the Marina Green.

Time Limit

At 11:00 AM all walkers and runners that are still on the course will be asked to move to the sidewalk to allow the roads to be re-opened to traffic. The finish line will be open until 12 PM and the Expo will be open until 1:00 PM. Sorry no dogs, bikes, roller skates, skate boards, scooters, etc allowed on the course. The race goes on rain or shine!

Timing & Results

Chip Timing

All registered runners will be timed by chip. The chip will be attached to each race bib. You'll need to remove the chip and attach it to your shoe (per the instructions on the chip).


Medals

All finishers will receive a 2016 Bridge to Bridge medal presented by a Special Olympics Athlete at the post race Expo, plus each finisher 21+ can enjoy one free beer and stick around after the race for the party.

The top three overall male and female in the both races will be determined by the gun/horn start. Age division winners will be scored by chip time. Results will be available race day at the expo and online following the race.
