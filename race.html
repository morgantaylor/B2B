San Francisco Originals Series

Register now for the final stop on the San Francisco Original Series tour - The Oral-B Glide Floss Bridge to Bridge Run.

This one's for you, San Francisco. If you're into running in a banana costume, running across the Golden Gate Bridge, and finishing on the field at AT&T Park - then get yourself eligible for the San Francisco Originals Run Series, back for its fourth year in 2016. It's a collaboration between San Francisco's most iconic running events, The Zappos.com Bay to Breakers, The San Francisco Marathon, The San Francisco Giant Race Presented by Blueprint for Athletes, and Glide Floss Bridge to Bridge.

For those of you with the endurance to finish all four, we'll hand you a big fat SFO Series medal at the finish line of Glide Floss Bridge to Bridge (see image below).


2016 SFO Series Medal

How To Become A San Francisco Original:

  • Register for and complete the Zappos.com Bay to Breakers (5/15), SF Marathon (7/31), Giant Race (9/11) and the Oral-B Glide Floss Bridge to Bridge Run (10/2). Please use the same name, email address, and birth date when registering.
  • - Click here to register for the final race of the series before Thursday, September 29th.
  • The first 2500 registrants will become eligible for the 2016 medal.
  • If you have ran Bay to Breakers, SF Marathon and Giant Race – consider yourself eligible! Now you just need to finish the Bridge to Bridge Run on Sunday, October 2nd.
  • You may run any distance in each of the four events to receive the medal. SF Originals Runners can pick up their series medal at the Oral-B Glide Floss Bridge to Bridge Run After Party at the “SFO Series Medal Redemption” booth!

Bibs & Timing

Will Call & Packet Pick Up

7AM–8:45AM @ Justin Herman Plaza across from the Ferry Building. If you haven’t received your bib number, pick it up at Will Call. Please arrive early. If you’ve lost your bib number, the volunteers at Will Call can help you.

Bib Number & Timing Chip

Bib # must be worn on front of shirt on the course in the finish chutes and for post race refreshments. The timing chip is on the back of your bib # - do not remove the timing chip or the bibs tear off tag at the bottom of your bib #.

Results

Medals

All finishers will receive a 2016 Bridge to Bridge medal presented by a Special Olympics Athlete at the post race After Party. Each finisher 21+ will also enjoy one free beer.

The top three overall male and female in the both races will be determined by the gun/horn start. Age division winners will be scored by chip time.

Race Results

Race results will be posted on race day at the After Party and online following the race finish.

Course Information


Both courses start at 9:00am downtown at the Ferry Building in the shadow of the Bay Bridge. Runners should line up according to mile pace signs to allow for a smooth and safe start. The course goes down the Embarcadero, through Fisherman's Wharf and along Marina Green where the 5K finishes. The 12K continues through Crissy Field with views of the Golden Gate Bridge, and finally back to Marina Green to finish.

Course Rules

Backpacks, large bags and any type of container/carrier exceeding 8.5” x 11” x 4” are not allowed on the race course. Runners/walkers should stay in designated runner lanes.

FAQs

Water

There will be a water station at Justin Herman Plaza.

There is one water station along the 5K route and three water stations along the 12K route. First is approximately 2.25 miles (on Van ness near the municipal pier). The second is approximately 4.5 miles at the west end of Crissy Field. This station also serves as the third water station as runners return from the 12K, approximately 5.5 miles.

There will be water tables after the finish lines.

Restrooms

Portable toilets are located at the start (Justin Herman Plaza) and After Party (Marina Green).

Strollers

We ask strollers to line up towards back of the starting line. One thing to be aware of the Shuttle Buses are Yellow School buses. There are a couple that are handicapped accessible which can accommodate strollers but sometimes there is a wait to catch the shuttle back to the start. If you have a stroller that folds it possibly can fit on the regular buses. You don’t need to register the child in the stroller unless you want them to get a finisher medal and goodie bag.

Changing Events

If you accidently signed up for the wrong event or have changed your mind and want to switch events please log back into Runsignup.com using the login that you created when registering for the race. From there navigate to "My Races" where you should see "Bridge to Bridge". Then change your event and pay the remaining diffence.

Medical

We hope you don’t need it, but if you do there are medical tents at Justin Herman Plaza and at the After Party at Marina Green. There will also be ambulances on standby at the start and finish. If you have a medical need while out on the course, let one of the course monitors know or ask another runner to find one for you.

Time Limit

At 11:00 AM all walkers and runners that are still on the course will be asked to move to the sidewalk to allow the roads to be re-opened to traffic. The finish line will be open until 12 PM and the Expo will be open until 1:00 PM. Sorry no dogs, bikes, roller skates, skate boards, scooters, etc allowed on the course. The race goes on rain or shine!

Gear Check

We will provide bags for transporting small personal items and articles of clothing only from the start area to the finish. If bringing your own bag it must be transparent and no larger than 12”x 12” x 7” and marked clearly with your bib number.

No backpacks, large bags or containers will be transported

Oral-B Glide Floss, race officials, and Cumulus radio representatives are not liable for any lost, damaged or stolen items. We strongly recommend that you do not place any valuable items (phones, cameras, keys, drivers licenses, etc) in your bag. This is a courtesy check. Please note: the last truck leaves at 8:40am.
